Hyperspace Rocket Designs and Tea Bar

We are a family owned and operated hobby store with an attached tea bar at TownMall of Westminster.

Flying Model Rockets, Flying Model Planes, Gundam Plastic Models (Gunpla), Scale Models, Working Catapults, Working Trebuchets, Hobby Supplies, Books, Premium Hot Tea, Bags of Premium Loose Leaf Tea, Coffee, Premium Ghirardelli Cocoa, and more.

We carry premium The Tea In Me™ teas. For a delicious treat, try our specialty flavors like Solar Tropics, Wine and Roses, Constellation Cookie, Toasted Startdust, and more.

Join us for build events every Tuesday from 5PM to 7PM. Bring your kits to work on, or buy a new kit while you are here. (Because we build at tables in the mall court, solvents and aerosols are not allowed at the build events.)

Visit us today at TownMall of Westminster!
